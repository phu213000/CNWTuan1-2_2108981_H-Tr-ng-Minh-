A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the side of the mouth . It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile . Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes , an action know as " Duchenne smile" . Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
